# E-Commerce-Profitability-Analysis-Dashboard

📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze the performance of an e-commerce business by tracking key business metrics such as Sales, Profit, Orders, Product Performance, Customer Segments, Shipping Modes, and Sales Trends.

The objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that help management understand overall business performance and support data-driven decision-making.

🎯 Business Problem

An e-commerce company has experienced consistent sales growth over multiple years, but management wants to better understand the factors influencing profitability.

This dashboard was created to answer key business questions such as:

Which product categories generate the highest profit?
Which products contribute the most to overall sales?
Which customer segment is the most profitable?
How does shipping mode affect profitability?
What is the relationship between discounts and profit?
How have sales changed over the years and across different months?
